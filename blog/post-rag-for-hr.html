Designing a RAG Assistant for HR Documents

Published for the portfolio — 5 min read

A strong enterprise assistant is not only about generating text. It is about retrieving the right information, citing the right source, and helping users find answers faster.

Key decisions

  • Chunk documents carefully to preserve meaning.
  • Use semantic retrieval and reranking when needed.
  • Keep access control and sensitive data in mind from day one.

Why it worked

The best results came from combining domain knowledge, clean metadata, and a simple user workflow. This reduced search time and made the system more useful than a generic chatbot.
